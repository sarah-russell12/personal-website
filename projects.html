Gambit of One

Description: My thesis project to graduate from New College of Florida. Completed January 2017. A small game that implments some of the basic features of an action RPG.
Skills: C++, XML, Object-Oriented Design, World Design, Character Design, Game Development
Features:
  • Ability to explore a 6 x 6 forest maze
  • Engage in melee and ranged combat with randomized damage
  • Fight rats, bandits, and archers
  • Gain experience from fighting enemies
  • Ability scores determine your damage and speed in combat
  • Level up to increase your ability scores
  • Defeat all the enemies in the forest to win
